ABOUT US

Our story began in 1985

What started as a small gathering in a living room has grown into a thriving community of believers passionate about making a difference.

Today, we're committed to authentic worship, biblical teaching, and serving our neighbors with the love of Christ.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

THE SCRIPTURE

The Bible is the inspired, inerrant word of God—our ultimate authority for faith and life.

THE TRINITY

God eternally exists as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—three persons, one God.

SALVATION

We are saved by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works.
