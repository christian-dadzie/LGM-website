ABOUT US

Our Story

From a living room to a movement—here's how The Lamb of God came to be

Started in 1996

What began as a handful of people meeting in a living room has grown into a thriving community of believers who are passionate about Jesus and committed to making a difference.

Today, we're still the same at heart—real people, real faith, real impact. We believe church should be a place where everyone belongs, grows, and serves.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

The Trinity

Grace
