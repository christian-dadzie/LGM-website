Our Heritage of Faith

For nearly four decades, The Lamb of God Ministry has been a cornerstone of faith and fellowship in Cityville

Our Story

Founded in 1996 by a small group of faithful believers, The Lamb of God Ministry began in a humble living room with a vision to create a place where all could experience God's transforming love.

Today, we continue that legacy as a vibrant, multigenerational congregation committed to worship, discipleship, and serving others in Jesus' name.

What We Believe

Holy Scripture

The Bible is God's inspired, inerrant Word—our foundation for faith and practice.

The Trinity

We worship one God eternally existing as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Salvation by Grace

We are saved through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by our own works.
