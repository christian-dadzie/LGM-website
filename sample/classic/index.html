Our Mission & Story

Since 1996, Lamb of God Ministries has been a beacon of hope in Darkuman. We are a family of believers dedicated to loving God and loving our neighbors with authenticity and purpose.

What We Believe

  • The Scripture: We believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant word of God and our final authority.
  • The Trinity: God eternally exists in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • Salvation: We are saved by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.
